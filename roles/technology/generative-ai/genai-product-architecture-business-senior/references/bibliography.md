# Bibliografia

Recursos de fondo para ampliar criterio del rol. No sustituyen fuentes vigentes cuando haya que decidir sobre modelos, precios, regulacion o seguridad actual.

## Normas, guias y regulacion

- NIST. `Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework: Generative Artificial Intelligence Profile` (NIST AI 600-1), 2024.
- NIST. `Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)`, 2023.
- OWASP Foundation. `OWASP Top 10 for Large Language Model Applications`, version 2025.
- ISO/IEC. `ISO/IEC 42001:2023 Information technology - Artificial intelligence - Management system`, 2023.
- European Union. `Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 laying down harmonised rules on artificial intelligence`, 2024.
- Microsoft. `Responsible AI Standard` y principios publicos de IA responsable.

## Investigacion y practica

- Noy, Shakked; Zhang, Whitney. `Experimental evidence on the productivity effects of generative artificial intelligence`, Science, 2023.
- Brynjolfsson, Erik; Li, Danielle; Raymond, Lindsey R. `Generative AI at Work`, Quarterly Journal of Economics, 2025.
- Amershi, Saleema et al. `Guidelines for Human-AI Interaction`, CHI, 2019.
- Google PAIR. `People + AI Guidebook`.
- OpenAI. Documentacion de API sobre produccion, evaluacion, guardrails, razonamiento, herramientas y agentes.
