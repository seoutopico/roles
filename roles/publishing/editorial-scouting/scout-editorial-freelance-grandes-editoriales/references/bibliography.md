# Bibliografia

- Williams, Emily. "Inside the Secret World of Literary Scouts." Publishing Perspectives, 2009-12-14. https://publishingperspectives.com/2009/12/inside-the-secret-world-of-literary-scouts/
- Williams, Emily. "Inside the Secret World of Literary Scouts, Part II." Publishing Perspectives, 2009-12-21. https://publishingperspectives.com/2009/12/inside-the-secret-world-of-literary-scouts-part-ii/
- Williams, Emily. "Inside the Secret World of Literary Scouts, Part III." Publishing Perspectives, 2010-01-04. https://publishingperspectives.com/2010/01/inside-the-secret-world-of-literary-scouts-part-iii/
- Penguin Random House Grupo Editorial. "Foreign Rights Catalogues." https://www.penguinrandomhousegrupoeditorial.com/foreign-rights/foreign-rights-catalogues/
- Penguin Random House Grupo Editorial. "Foreign Rights Contacts." https://www.penguinrandomhousegrupoeditorial.com/foreign-rights/fr-contacts/
- Frankfurter Buchmesse. Home and rights context. https://www.buchmesse.de/en
- The London Book Fair. "The International Rights Centre." https://www.londonbookfair.co.uk/en-gb/visit/international-rights-centre-visitor.html
- Bologna Children's Book Fair. Home, Rights Centre and Global Rights Exchange context. https://www.bolognachildrensbookfair.com/en/home/878.html

## Fuentes recomendadas para uso continuo

- Catalogos de foreign rights de PRHGE, Planeta, Hachette, HarperCollins, Simon & Schuster, Macmillan y editoriales independientes relevantes.
- Publishers Marketplace, Publishers Lunch, The Bookseller, Publishers Weekly, Publishing Perspectives y Literary Hub para deals, movimientos de editores, agentes y tendencias.
- Informes de Nielsen BookScan, Circana, GfK, FGEE, Publishers Association, IPA y asociaciones nacionales cuando el cliente tenga acceso.
- Programas, directorios y derechos de Frankfurt, London Book Fair, Bologna, Guadalajara, Sharjah y ferias regionales relevantes.

## Nota

Las fuentes de pago o privadas no se deben simular. Si el usuario no aporta acceso, el rol debe pedir datos o marcar el punto como no verificado.
